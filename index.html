Organization Simulation-- In the simulation, ORGANIZE the choices in a way that feels right to you...

What is this Study About?


This study observes the extent of how high school adolescents' organization of a set of pictures correlates with their self-perceived personality traits.
For this portion of the study, you will be organizing a set of rectangular pictures.


  • You may opt out of this study/simulation at any time by pressing the QUIT button on the top.
  • Once you click start, you will have 25 seconds to organize the choices the best that you see fit. There will be a timer at the top of the screen.

    • 1. Once you start the simulation, there will be 8 rectangular boxes to select from at the bottom. There will also be a gallery display at the top to show your selections (from left to right).

    2. You can hover to enlarge the rectangular boxes for a more clear image.

    3. If you misclick, you can remove all selections with the restart button at the top.
